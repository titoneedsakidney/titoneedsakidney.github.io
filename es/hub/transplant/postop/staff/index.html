Transplant Staff - Kidney Transplant Hub

Post-Op Staff

After transplant, this crew watches your labs, tunes medicines, and helps you get back to normal life. Meet the people you’ll see most often.

Transplant Nephrologist

Leads medical care after transplant and adjusts anti-rejection meds based on labs and symptoms.

Transplant Pharmacist

Checks drug levels, looks for interactions, teaches timing (e.g., trough labs), and helps with refills/PA.

Nurse Coordinator

Your first call for symptoms, scheduling, refills, and “who do I call?” triage.

Dietitian

Food safety, protein/calorie goals, salt and sugar plans, and lab-guided tweaks (potassium, magnesium).

Social Worker

Coping support, work/benefits paperwork, caregiver resources, and practical problem-solving.

Informational only; roles vary by center.
