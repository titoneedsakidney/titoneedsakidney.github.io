Start Here

This site exists to make life with kidney failure less confusing and less lonely.

If you or someone you care about is dealing with dialysis, transplant, or donation — this is for you.

You’ll find:

  • Pages that explain the process step by step
  • Testimonials from real people — both donors and recipients
  • Simple explanations of complicated things (like lab work or access options)
  • Personal stories about the things no one warned us about

Start wherever makes sense for you:

  • Donor Info — facts about living donation
  • Dialysis Hub — what to expect, and how to manage it
  • Transplant Hub — how evaluation works, what recovery is really like

There’s no popups or login. Just real info. If you’re overwhelmed, take a break and come back later. It’ll still be here.
