Post-Op Staff: Dietitian

Role at a glance

  • Helps food work with your meds and labs, not against them.
  • Focus on food safety, energy, blood pressure, and weight.

They handle

  • Food safety guidelines during immunosuppression.
  • Protein and calorie goals for healing and strength.
  • Salt reduction for blood pressure; sugar strategies if steroids raise glucose.
  • Electrolyte tips (e.g., potassium/magnesium) based on labs.

Not their job

  • They don’t prescribe or change medicines; they align diet with your plan.

When to see them / what to bring

  • Early after transplant and whenever weight, BP, or labs drift.
  • Bring a 3-day food log (photos are fine) and any supplements.

Questions to ask: What should I change first? Any foods that interact with my meds? How much protein and sodium should I aim for?

Educational info; follow your team’s plan.
