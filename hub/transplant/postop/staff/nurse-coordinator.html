Post-Op Staff: Nurse Coordinator

Role at a glance

  • Your first call for symptoms, questions, and scheduling.
  • Connects you to the right person fast.

They handle

  • Triage for fever, GI upset, wound questions, and medication issues.
  • Ordering labs per protocol; scheduling visits, imaging, or biopsy.
  • Refill requests and messaging with your providers.

Not their job

  • They don’t make final diagnoses or set med doses—but they get you to the provider who does.

When to see them / what to bring

  • Any new/worsening symptom; questions about instructions; paperwork help.
  • Bring your med list, BP/weight logs, and recent lab dates.

Questions to ask: What is “urgent” vs. “send a message”? Who is on call after hours? How do I prep for a trough lab?

Educational info; follow your team’s plan.
