Transplant Staff - Kidney Transplant Hub

Pre-Op Staff

This team clears you for surgery, coordinates tests, and prepares you for a safe recovery.

Transplant Coordinator

Your main contact for scheduling, instructions, and getting questions to the right person.

Transplant Nephrologist

Reviews test results, tunes your plan, and clears you for surgery with the team.

Transplant Surgeon (Consult)

Explains the operation, incision, stent/drain plans, and answers technical questions.

Social Worker

Coping, caregiver planning, work/benefits, and resource referrals.

Financial Coordinator

Insurance, authorizations, out-of-pocket estimates, and medication coverage help.

Informational only; roles vary by center.
