Labs, heart and lung checks, imaging, and consults to be sure surgery is safe and the match is right.

Realistic look at surgical risks, infection risk later, and how the team lowers those risks.

Who does what before surgery—coordinator, nephrologist, surgeon (consult), social worker, financial coordinator.
