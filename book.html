Tito Needs a Kidney (The Book)

“Tito Needs a Kidney” is a firsthand account of what it’s really like to survive kidney failure — told with clarity, honesty, and a refusal to sugarcoat what matters.

The book covers:

  • What dialysis actually feels like
  • What it means to lose your independence and fight to get it back
  • How transplant really works (and what people often get wrong)
  • Why the donor process is confusing — and how to make it better
  • How one guy navigated it all without giving up on living fully

This book is for:

  • People on dialysis or newly diagnosed with kidney disease
  • Donors who want to understand the whole picture
  • Families who want to help but don’t know how
  • Anyone who needs to see what hope actually looks like in practice
